a little about me:

My name is Julissa Zavala and I am a UX designer and mixed media artist living in New York City. I enjoy creating products and services that provide inclusive user experiences and inventive solutions to everyday problems.
‍
Having a background as an artist and educator gives me a unique perspective as a UX designer. As an artist, I have always been a creative person driven to find unique solutions to problems, whether they exist in my art practice or in the real world. As an educator, I am compelled to help people of all ages, equipped with research principles I learned in college for child behavior and human development. These facets of who I am help me approach design problems with an empathic mindset ready to create innovative solutions.
‍
When I’m not designing, I enjoy painting, trying out new recipes and playing with my dog. I also enjoy connecting with other people who are just as passionate about UX as I am. If that’s you, let’s connect!
